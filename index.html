Crystal Collector!

You'll be given a random number at the start of the game.


There will be four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points for your total score.


You will win the game by matching your total score to random number, you loose game if your total score goes above a random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of the crystals.
